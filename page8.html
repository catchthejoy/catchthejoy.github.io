- 送餸 BuyBuyFresh -

抱・快樂 與合作夥伴 - 香港聖公會東涌綜合服務中心推出買餸手機應用程式，鼓勵方便自主、營養和價錢三方面的平衡，並透過涌時券，鼓勵及凝聚社區不同人士的參與，團結力量實踐「人盡其才、物盡其用」的模式，重建互助社區，達至改善生活。

Catch the Joy, together with HKSKH Tung Chung Integrated Services, will introduce a mobile application to serve as your shopping helper. It aims at striving the balance between convenience, nutrition intakes, and financial burden. By using 'Chung Coupon', people from different walks of life are encouraged to take part in our project, thereby to increase social cohesion. The togetherness we create leads to “the showcase of each and every talent, and the utilization of our resources to the fullest”. These improve people’s lives and create a caring community.

“抱緊多一點 從身邊擦過的快樂”  

抱・快樂 是由一群關心社會的年青人自發組成的慈善團隊，透過舉辦不同的活動項目及機構合作，希望幫助香港人找回生活中單純的快樂片段，透過分享和互相感染，將快樂遍佈至社區每一個角落，令這個充滿人情味的地方多點溫度。

Catch the Joy is a charitable organization founded by a group of young people, who strives for a better society to live in. By organizing different projects and working with other organisation, we would like to bring back the simple and joyful moments in life to people in Hong Kong. We will spread warmth and friendliness to every corner in society through sharing with others.

  - 我們的使命 Our Aims-

為弱勢社群提供多一點關心和愛

To care and to love the needy

為社區注入正能量，加強人際間的連繫

To bring positivity and to build stronger bonds within a community

為有需要的學童提供增值活動，以教育改善跨代貧窮問題

To alleviate transgenerational poverty with the help of education
